Just Keep Moving is all about making a better you through displacement, inspiring people to forget their chairs, go outside and MOVE! Cycling, jogging, swimming, hiking, walking... it's all movement and all can be fun. How does JKM do this, by sharing your adventures whether they come in the form of an image of the routes taken while cycling, pictures and videos of events seen while running, or the simple personal update stating "I feel good!".

JKM also offers ways of keeping track of your activities, goals set and attained, records broken and other statistics. How about needing a walking buddy, or planning a cycling expedition? Well, JKM assists by pairing you with people of similar interests, level and schedules facilitating social interaction and a good time.
